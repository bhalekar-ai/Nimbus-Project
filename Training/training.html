Kick-Start Your Offshore Career
With Atlas Professionals

Atlas Professionals In Aberdeen Initiated The Greenhand Programme In August 2018 For Those Interested In Going Offshore For The First Time. Since Then, Hundreds Of Professionals Have Found Their Way Into The Offshore Industry.

The Very First Greenhand Course Was Held In Atlas Professionals Aberdeen Office In July 2018 With The First Professional Being Selected By A Client For An Offshore Position That Same Day. The Aberdeen Drilling Team Then Went On To Recruit And Mobilise Over 100 Greenhand Roustabouts Between 2018 And March 2020. With The COVID-19 Pandemic Impacting The Offshore Industry Heavily After The Second Quarter Of 2020, The Programme Was Put On Hold To Assess How To Proceed In A Safe And Reliable Manner.

In May 2021, However, The Programme Was Re-Started With Courses Being Held At The Premises Of The Training Provider Within Provided Safety Guidelines. Since Then, The Greenhand Programme Has Managed To Complete One Milestone After The Other With The Atlas’ Aberdeen Office Recently Celebrating Mobilisation Of The 675th Greenhand Roustabout.

Greenhand Induction Day

Atlas Professionals Will Be Carrying Out Greenhand Induction Days And At These Days Attendees Will Have A One-To-One Interview, Aptitude Tests, Receive Advice On Life Offshore And Given Safety Awareness Training By Atlas Professionals Health And Safety Manager Who Is Also An Experienced Offshore Safety Officer. The Interactive Day Will Consist Of:

  • Drilling Mastery Class / Process Safety
  • Life Offshore
  • Risk Management Tools
  • Lock Out Tag Out
  • Lifting Operations And Control Of Lifting Equipment
  • COSHH Awareness
  • Stop Work Authority
  • In The Line Of Fire (Red Zone Awareness)
  • Prevention Of Hand And Finger Injuries
  • Prevention Of Dropped Objects/Working At Height
  • Barrier Management
  • Where The Oil And Gas Industry Is Currently

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Your Question Not There? Please Email Us.

How Do I Attend A Greenhand Training Day?

Register For One Of Our Upcoming Greenhand Training Days And Complete Your Atlas Account. Your Application Will Then Be Reviewed.

What Happens During The Greenhand Training Day?

You Will Undergo Safety Awareness Training With Our Health & Safety Manager Mark McDonald As Well As The Overview Of Greenhand Programme Recruitment Process & Co-Ordination From The Greenhand Account Manager Liam Connell. You Will Also Complete An Aptitude Test.

Where Does The Training Take Place?

We Currently Organise The Greenhand Training Days In Aberdeen And Newcastle, United Kingdom.

How Long Is The Training?

It Is A One-Day Training Course.

What Job Can I Be Offered As A Result Of Greenhand Training?

Once All Relevant Courses Are Passed, Atlas Professionals Aberdeen Will Be Able To Spec Out Your CV To Our Clients For Greenhand Roustabout Opportunities. We Have Both Rotational And Ad Hoc Opportunities Across A Vast Array Of Clients Available. However, Please Note That Atlas Professionals Cannot Guarantee That You Will Be Offered A Role On The Completion Of These Courses As Opportunities Are Very Much Client And Market Driven.

How Much Does It Cost To Attend?

We Offer Our Greenhand Trainings Days Free Of Charge.

What Training Certificates Do I Need?

To Gain Employment As A Greenhand Roustabout, You Will Require The Following:

  • BOSIET With CA EBS
  • OGUK Medical
  • MIST
  • Shoulder Measurements
  • Banksman Slinger Stage 1 (Initial)
